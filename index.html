The relationship

The longer you use Fern,
the more irreplaceable she becomes.

1

Week 1 — Getting to know you

Remi learns your name, nickname, household, cuisines you love, how much time you have. The conversation begins.

2

Month 1 — Starting to know you

She notices you cook Italian three nights a week. She knows Wednesday is busy. She stops suggesting complex recipes mid-week.

3

Month 3 — She really knows you

"Kiki, it's Tuesday. Soccer night. I have a 20-minute pasta that's been in your saved list for two weeks." That's it. That's Remi.

Month 6+ — Indispensable
